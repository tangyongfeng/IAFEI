About IAFEI

International Association of Financial Executives Institutes

Our Mission

To advance the profession of financial management through education, networking, and the development of best practices among financial executives worldwide.

Excellence

Committed to the highest standards of professional competence and ethical conduct.

Innovation

Fostering innovative approaches to financial challenges and emerging industry trends.

Collaboration

Building global networks that enable knowledge sharing and professional growth.

Our Vision

To be the premier global association for financial executives, setting the standard for professional excellence and ethical leadership in financial management worldwide.

Our Objectives

Professional Development

Enhance the knowledge, skills, and capabilities of financial executives through continuing education, training programs, and professional certification.

Standards & Ethics

Establish and maintain high professional and ethical standards for financial executives and promote their adoption worldwide.

Knowledge Exchange

Facilitate the exchange of ideas, best practices, and innovative solutions among financial professionals globally.

Global Networking

Build and strengthen professional networks that span across countries, industries, and organizational levels.

Research & Innovation

Promote research and development in financial management practices and emerging technologies.

Organizational Structure

Executive Committee

The governing body responsible for strategic direction and policy decisions, comprising elected officers from member institutes worldwide.
