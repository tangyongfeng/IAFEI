Committee Overview

The IAFEI Technical Committee serves as the primary body for developing and disseminating best practices in financial management, accounting standards, and emerging technologies that impact the profession globally.

Committee Membership

The Technical Committee comprises distinguished financial executives, academics, and subject matter experts from IAFEI member organizations worldwide. Members serve three-year terms and contribute their expertise to advance the profession.

Membership Criteria

  • Extensive experience in financial management or related fields
  • Recognized expertise in specific technical areas
  • Commitment to advancing professional standards and practices
  • Active participation in committee activities and initiatives
