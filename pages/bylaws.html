IAFEI By-Laws

Governing principles and operational framework of the International Association of Financial Executives Institutes

Introduction

The By-Laws of the International Association of Financial Executives Institutes (IAFEI) establish the fundamental principles, governance structure, and operational procedures that guide our organization in advancing financial excellence worldwide.

Article I - Membership

Section 1 - Eligibility

Membership in IAFEI is open to national and regional associations of financial executives that demonstrate commitment to professional excellence and ethical standards in financial management.

Section 2 - Member Obligations

Member organizations shall maintain professional standards, participate in IAFEI activities, and contribute to the advancement of financial management practices within their respective regions.

Article II - Governance Structure

Section 1 - Board of Directors

The Board of Directors shall consist of representatives from member organizations, including a Chairman, Vice-Chairman, and regional representatives, elected for specified terms as outlined in the governance procedures.

Section 2 - Officers

IAFEI officers include the Chairman, Vice-Chairman, Secretary, Treasurer, and regional representatives, each with specific responsibilities for organizational leadership and regional coordination.

Article III - World Congress

The IAFEI World Congress shall convene annually, rotating among member regions, to facilitate knowledge sharing, professional development, and strategic planning for the advancement of financial management practices globally.

Article IV - Committees

IAFEI may establish technical committees and working groups to address specific aspects of financial management, professional development, and industry challenges as deemed necessary by the Board of Directors.

Article V - Amendments

These By-Laws may be amended by a majority vote of the Board of Directors, with proposed amendments circulated to all member organizations at least 30 days prior to the vote.
