IAFEI Announces 2025 World Congress Theme

January 15, 2025 | Manila, Philippines

IAFEI is pleased to announce that the theme for the 2025 World Congress will be "Digital Finance: Transforming the Future of Financial Leadership." The congress will explore how artificial intelligence, blockchain technology, and digital transformation are reshaping the role of financial executives worldwide.

Expected to attract over 300 financial executives from more than 20 countries, the event will feature keynote presentations from industry leaders, technical sessions on emerging technologies, and networking opportunities for professional development.

New ESG Reporting Guidelines Released

December 20, 2024 | Global

IAFEI's Technical Committee has published comprehensive guidelines for Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) reporting. These guidelines provide financial executives with practical frameworks for implementing sustainable reporting practices and meeting increasing stakeholder demands for transparency.

The guidelines cover materiality assessment, data collection methodologies, and disclosure best practices, helping organizations navigate the complex landscape of sustainability reporting.

Partnership with Global Universities Announced

December 10, 2024 | Global

IAFEI has entered into strategic partnerships with leading business schools worldwide to enhance professional development opportunities for financial executives. The partnerships will facilitate executive education programs, research collaboration, and knowledge exchange.

Initial partners include institutions from Asia, Europe, and the Americas, providing global reach for professional development initiatives.
