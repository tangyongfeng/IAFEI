Membership Benefits

Global Network

Access to a worldwide community of financial professionals and thought leaders in corporate finance.

Professional Development

Continuing education opportunities, certifications, and training programs to enhance professional skills.

Knowledge Sharing

Exchange of best practices, research findings, and innovative solutions in financial management.

World Congress

Annual international conference featuring industry experts, networking opportunities, and technical sessions.

Publications

Access to IAFEI Quarterly, research reports, and professional guidance documents.

Standards Development

Participation in the development of professional standards and ethical guidelines for financial executives.

How to Join IAFEI

IAFEI membership is through national or regional financial executives institutes. If your country or region has an existing member institute, you can join IAFEI through that organization. If there is no member institute in your area, we welcome inquiries about establishing new member institutes.

Requirements for Member Institutes

  • Established organization representing financial executives
  • Commitment to professional standards and ethical conduct
  • Active membership base and governance structure
  • Ability to participate in IAFEI activities and contribute to objectives
