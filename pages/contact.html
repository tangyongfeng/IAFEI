Frequently Asked Questions

How can I become a member of IAFEI?

IAFEI membership is through national or regional member institutes. If your country has a member institute, you can join through them. If not, contact us about establishing a new member institute in your region.

When and where is the next World Congress?

The IAFEI World Congress 2025 will be held October 15-17, 2025, in Manila, Philippines. The theme is "Digital Finance: Transforming the Future of Financial Leadership."

How can I access IAFEI publications?

IAFEI publications are available to members through our digital platform. Non-members can subscribe to specific publications or request access through their local member institute.

Does IAFEI offer professional certifications?

IAFEI works with member institutes to provide professional development and certification programs. Contact your local member institute or our secretariat for specific certification opportunities.

Can I submit articles for IAFEI publications?

Yes, we welcome contributions from financial professionals worldwide. Please review our editorial guidelines and submission process by contacting our publications team.
