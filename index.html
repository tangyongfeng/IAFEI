What is IAFEI?

The International Association of Financial Executives Institutes (IAFEI) is dedicated to advancing the profession of financial management worldwide. Our mission is to promote excellence in financial leadership, facilitate knowledge exchange, and drive innovation in corporate finance practices.

Since our establishment in 1969, IAFEI has been the premier platform for financial executives, CFOs, and organizations to share best practices, discuss emerging trends, and shape the future of financial management across industries and borders.
